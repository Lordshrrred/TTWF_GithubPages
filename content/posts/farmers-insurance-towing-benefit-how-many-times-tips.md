> **Quick Answer:** Farmers Insurance typically allows 3-4 towing service calls per policy year, depending on your coverage level. Basic roadside assistance usually covers 3 calls, while enhanced plans may offer 4 or more. Check your policy declarations page for exact limits.

## What To Do

1. **Check your policy documents** for the exact number of towing calls allowed per year
2. **Call Farmers at 1-800-435-7764** to verify remaining uses on your current policy period
3. **Request towing service** through the Farmers mobile app or by calling the roadside assistance number
4. **Keep track of usage** by saving service confirmation numbers and dates
5. **Consider upgrading coverage** if you frequently need towing services

## What Your Policy Typically Covers

**Standard Roadside Assistance:**
- 3 towing calls per policy year
- Up to 15 miles towing distance
- Resets on your policy renewal date

**Enhanced Coverage Options:**
- 4+ towing calls per year
- Extended towing distances (up to 100 miles)
- Higher reimbursement limits

**Usage Resets:**
- Annual reset on policy renewal date
- Not calendar year based
- Unused calls don't roll over

## What It Might Cost

- **With coverage:** $0 per incident (within policy limits)
- **After limits exceeded:** $75-150 per tow depending on distance
- **Coverage upgrade:** Usually $20-40 per year additional premium

## Stay Safe

• Don't wait to call if you've used previous tows this year
• Verify your remaining benefits before requesting service
• Keep your policy number and VIN handy when calling
• Consider the distance to avoid unexpected charges
• Never assume you have coverage without checking first
