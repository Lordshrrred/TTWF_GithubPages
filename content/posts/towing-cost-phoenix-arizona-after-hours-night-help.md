> **Quick Answer:** Phoenix after-hours towing runs $150-300+ for standard cars at night (6 PM to 6 AM). Distance, vehicle size, and specific company policies drive the final price. Weekends and holidays cost extra. Always confirm total price before agreeing to service.

## What To Do

1. **Call multiple companies immediately.** Get quotes from at least 3 tow services before choosing. Prices vary dramatically between operators.

2. **Ask for the complete breakdown.** Base rate plus mileage, after-hours surcharge, and any hidden fees. Don't accept vague estimates.

3. **Confirm your exact location.** Use GPS coordinates or nearby landmarks. Wrong locations mean extra charges and delays.

4. **Know your vehicle details.** Year, make, model, and whether it runs. Non-running cars cost more to load.

5. **Verify the destination distance.** Most companies charge $3-7 per mile after the first 5-10 miles.

6. **Get arrival time estimates.** After-hours services often take 45-90 minutes in Phoenix metro area.

## What It Might Cost

**Standard sedan/SUV (running):**
- Base hook-up: $75-125
- After-hours surcharge: $25-75
- Mileage: $3-7 per mile
- Total typical range: $150-300

**Additional charges:**
- Non-running vehicle: +$25-50
- Accident recovery: +$50-150
- Weekend/holiday premium: +$25-100
- Winch-out service: +$75-200

**Major Phoenix towing companies** typically charge similar base rates, but surcharges vary widely.

## Stay Safe

• Turn on hazard lights and exit vehicle safely
• Stand away from traffic, behind barriers when possible
• Keep doors unlocked for driver access to neutral/parking brake
• Have payment ready (most accept cards, some cash only)
• Take photos of your car's condition before towing
• Get driver's name, company info, and receipt with total charges
• Verify tow truck operator credentials and company markings
