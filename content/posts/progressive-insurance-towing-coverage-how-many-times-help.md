> **Quick Answer:** Progressive's roadside assistance has no annual limit on towing incidents. You can use it every time you need help, but each tow is limited to 15 miles and costs $30-50 per incident depending on your coverage level. Multiple tows in one day for the same breakdown count as one incident.

## What Progressive Towing Coverage Includes

Progressive offers roadside assistance as an add-on to your auto policy. Here's what you get:

**Basic Roadside Coverage ($30/year):**
- Towing up to 15 miles
- Battery jump-start
- Flat tire change
- Lockout service
- Emergency fuel delivery

**Enhanced Roadside Coverage ($50/year):**
- Towing up to 100 miles
- All basic services
- Winching service

## How Many Times You Can Use It

1. **No annual usage limits** - Use towing as often as needed throughout your policy period
2. **Each breakdown is one incident** - Even if you need multiple attempts to tow the same car on the same day
3. **New breakdowns reset the count** - Each separate mechanical failure starts fresh
4. **Coverage renews annually** - Your usage resets when you renew your policy

## What It Might Cost

- Basic coverage: $30/year, covers towing up to 15 miles
- Enhanced coverage: $50/year, covers towing up to 100 miles
- Distance overage: You pay extra for miles beyond your coverage limit
- No deductible for covered services

## Important Limitations

Progressive roadside assistance won't cover:
- Commercial vehicles or motorcycles (separate coverage needed)
- Vehicles used for ride-sharing during active trips
- Intentional damage or racing incidents
- Towing from private property without owner permission

## How to Request Towing

1. Call Progressive's 24/7 roadside number: 1-800-776-4737
2. Provide your policy number and location
3. Describe your vehicle's problem
4. Wait for the assigned tow truck (typically 30-60 minutes)
5. Show your driver's license to the tow operator
