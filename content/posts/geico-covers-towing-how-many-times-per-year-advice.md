> **Quick Answer:** GEICO typically covers towing 4 times per year with their Emergency Road Service (ERS) coverage. Each service call has a maximum benefit amount, usually around $100 per incident. Coverage resets annually on your policy renewal date.

## What To Do

1. **Check your policy documents** or GEICO app to confirm your exact ERS coverage details
2. **Call GEICO at 1-800-424-3426** if you need immediate towing service
3. **Stay with your vehicle** until the tow truck arrives if it's safe to do so
4. **Keep receipts** if you pay out of pocket and need reimbursement later
5. **Ask the tow operator** about costs upfront if your coverage limit might be exceeded

## Coverage Details

GEICO's Emergency Road Service includes:
- **4 service calls per policy year** (not calendar year)
- **Up to $100 per service call** in most states
- **15-mile towing radius** typically covered
- **Additional services** like jump starts, flat tire changes, lockout assistance

Your policy year runs from your renewal date, not January 1st. If you've used all 4 calls, you'll pay out of pocket until your policy renews.

## What It Might Cost

- **ERS coverage**: Usually $15-25 per year added to your premium
- **Without coverage**: $75-200 per tow depending on distance and location
- **Over the limit**: You pay the difference if towing exceeds your $100 benefit

## Stay Safe

- Pull completely off the roadway when possible
- Turn on hazard lights immediately
- Stay inside your vehicle if traffic is heavy
- Stand away from traffic if you must exit
- Have your policy number ready when calling
