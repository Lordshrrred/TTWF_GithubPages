> **Quick Answer:** Farmers Insurance covers towing up to your policy limits if you have comprehensive or collision coverage, or purchased roadside assistance. File your claim within 24-72 hours with receipts, photos, and incident details. Reimbursement typically takes 5-10 business days after approval.

## What To Do

1. **Check your coverage immediately.** Log into your Farmers app or call 1-888-327-6335 to verify you have comprehensive, collision, or roadside assistance coverage that includes towing.

2. **Keep all paperwork.** Save your towing receipt, any repair estimates, and take photos of your vehicle's condition and location where it broke down.

3. **File your claim fast.** Report the incident within 24-72 hours through the Farmers app, website, or by calling their claims line at 1-800-435-7764.

4. **Provide complete information.** Include the exact breakdown location, time, cause of breakdown, towing company name, distance towed, and total cost.

5. **Submit required documents.** Upload or email your towing receipt, any photos, police report (if applicable), and repair shop estimates if the vehicle needs work.

6. **Follow up if needed.** Check your claim status through the app or call your adjuster directly if you don't hear back within 5 business days.

## What It Might Cost

Farmers typically covers towing costs up to these limits:
- **Basic roadside assistance:** $100-150 per incident
- **Comprehensive/collision claims:** Actual towing costs as part of covered repairs
- **Enhanced roadside:** Up to $200-300 depending on your policy

Your deductible applies to comprehensive or collision claims, but not to standalone roadside assistance coverage.

## Stay Safe

• Never leave your broken-down vehicle unattended without proper warning devices
• Get the tow truck driver's license and insurance information
• Verify the towing company is legitimate before allowing them to hook up your car
• Take photos of any vehicle damage before and after towing
• Keep your insurance card and policy number easily accessible in your phone
