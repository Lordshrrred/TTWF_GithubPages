> **Quick Answer:** Liberty Mutual's standard roadside assistance covers towing up to 15 miles from your breakdown location. Enhanced coverage may extend this to 100 miles or more, depending on your specific policy. Check your policy documents or call Liberty Mutual directly to confirm your exact towing distance limit.

## What To Do

1. **Check your current policy documents** for your specific towing distance limit. Look for "Emergency Road Service" or "Roadside Assistance" sections.

2. **Call Liberty Mutual at 1-800-290-8711** if you can't locate your policy details. Have your policy number ready.

3. **Know your coverage level**:
   - Basic coverage: typically 15 miles
   - Enhanced coverage: usually 100+ miles
   - Some policies offer unlimited towing distance

4. **If you need towing beyond your limit**:
   - Pay the overage fee (usually $3-5 per additional mile)
   - Ask the tow truck driver for an exact quote before proceeding
   - Consider having your car towed to the nearest qualified repair shop within your limit

5. **Request towing to the nearest appropriate facility** if your preferred shop exceeds your distance limit. Liberty Mutual covers towing to the closest repair facility that can handle your specific vehicle type.

6. **Keep your policy number and Liberty Mutual's roadside number (1-800-290-8711)** saved in your phone for quick access during breakdowns.

## What It Might Cost

Standard Liberty Mutual roadside assistance typically costs $15-25 per year as an add-on to your auto insurance. If you exceed your towing distance limit, expect to pay $3-7 per additional mile beyond your covered distance.

Enhanced roadside packages with longer towing distances cost $30-50 annually but can save hundreds if you need long-distance towing.

## Stay Safe

- Pull completely off the roadway before calling for help
- Turn on hazard lights and set up flares or reflective triangles if you have them
- Stay inside your vehicle if you're on a busy highway
- Never accept rides from strangers while waiting for your tow truck
- Keep your doors locked and windows cracked for ventilation while waiting
