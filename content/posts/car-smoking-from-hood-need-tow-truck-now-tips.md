> **Emergency Response:** Stop driving instantly, shut down the engine, and get out of your vehicle. Never lift the hood while smoke is visible. Contact professional towing services immediately. Hood smoke signals critical engine issues including overheating, fluid leaks, or potential electrical fires requiring expert intervention.

## Immediate Action Steps

When you notice smoke billowing from your hood, follow this priority sequence:

1. **Get to safety** by moving to the roadside or nearest parking area
2. **Kill the engine** and extract your keys completely
3. **Evacuate the car** and distance yourself from the vehicle
4. **Keep the hood closed** regardless of curiosity about the source
5. **Contact emergency services** if flames are visible or electrical burning odors are present
6. **Arrange towing services** for situations without active fire
7. **Maintain safe distance** until professional help arrives
8. **Document the scene** with photos when circumstances permit

## Critical Safety Guidelines

Protect yourself and others during this automotive emergency:

- Resist investigating under a smoking hood
- Carry automotive fire suppression equipment when possible
- Position yourself upwind from any smoke
- Pre-program emergency contacts into your mobile device
- Store roadside service information in accessible locations
- Create distance between yourself and passing traffic
- Activate emergency flashers before leaving your vehicle
- Notify family members about your whereabouts and situation
