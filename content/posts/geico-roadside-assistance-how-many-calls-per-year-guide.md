> **Quick Answer:** GEICO roadside assistance has no annual limit on the number of service calls you can make. You can use jump starts, flat tire changes, lockout help, and other covered services as many times as needed per year. However, towing has specific dollar limits and distance restrictions that vary by coverage level.

## What To Do

1. **Check your GEICO policy documents** to confirm you have roadside assistance coverage. Not all policies include it automatically.

2. **Call GEICO directly at 1-800-424-3426** when you need roadside help. Save this number in your phone now.

3. **Understand what counts as unlimited**: Jump starts, flat tire changes, lockout assistance, and minor mechanical help have no call limits.

4. **Know your towing limits**: [GEICO covers towing](https://towwiththeflow.com/geico-roadside-assistance-towing-coverage-how-much-pays/) up to $100 per incident for basic coverage, or higher amounts if you purchased enhanced coverage.

5. **Keep receipts** if you pay out of pocket first. GEICO will reimburse covered services up to policy limits.

## How Many Calls You Get

GEICO does not limit the number of roadside assistance calls per year. You can use these services unlimited times:

- Battery jump starts
- Flat tire changes (if you have a spare)
- Lockout assistance
- Emergency fuel delivery (up to 2 gallons)
- Minor mechanical adjustments

Towing works differently. While you can request towing as many times as needed, each incident has dollar limits. Basic coverage typically covers $100 per tow, while enhanced coverage may cover $200 or more per incident.

If your car needs multiple tows in one year, each separate breakdown counts as a new incident with fresh dollar limits. A tow from your breakdown location to a repair shop, then later from the shop to your home, would be two separate incidents.

## When You Might Pay Extra

You'll pay out of pocket when costs exceed GEICO's coverage limits:

- Towing distances beyond covered miles (usually 15-25 miles)
- Towing costs above dollar limits
- Specialized towing for modified vehicles
- Weekend or holiday premium charges that exceed coverage

If you frequently need expensive towing services, compare [GEICO's coverage limits with other options](https://towwiththeflow.com/progressive-insurance-towing-coverage-how-many-times/) to see if different insurance roadside assistance might save money.

## Coverage Varies by State

GEICO roadside assistance terms can vary by state. Some states mandate specific coverage levels or service types. Check your policy documents or call GEICO to confirm your exact coverage limits and included services.

Enhanced roadside packages typically cost $15-25 more per year but provide higher towing dollar limits and longer distance coverage. If you drive an older car or live in a rural area, the upgrade often pays for itself with just one long-distance tow.

Remember that using GEICO roadside assistance typically does not affect your insurance rates or claims history, since roadside calls are considered a separate service benefit rather than insurance claims.
