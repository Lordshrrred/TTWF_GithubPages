## About Towing Costs By City

This site exists because finding real towing cost information online is harder than it should be.

Most results you find are vague, outdated, or written by people who've never dealt with a tow truck bill. We cover specific cities with realistic price ranges, what actually affects the cost, and how to avoid getting overcharged.

Every post covers:

- **Realistic base fees** for that city and region
- **Per-mile rates** and how distance drives total cost
- **After-hours and highway premiums** that catch drivers off guard
- **How to avoid getting ripped off** with specific, actionable advice
- **Local notes** on impound systems, state regulations, and city-specific programs

We update this site regularly as towing rates shift with local cost of living.
